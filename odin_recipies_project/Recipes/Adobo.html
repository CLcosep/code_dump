Adobo Recipe

The Adobo or Filipino braised pork is an old filipino dish that originates back during the spanish rule.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Pork or Meat of choice
  • dark soy sauce
  • brown sugar
  • pepper
  • pepper leaf
  • oil
  • garlic
  • star anise
  • black beans (optional)

Directions

  1. boil the meat and let it simmer
  2. Once the water evaporates add oil and garlic and let it cook for a bit
  3. Once the garlic is golden brown add the wet ingredients along with the spices
  4. stir and let it simmer again to thicken the sauce
  5. Once the sauce is thick plate it and done!
